Toronto Tamagotchi Club Logo Proposals

Hello, Toronto Tamaheads! Here are some logo ideas for your consideration. There are several different variations, but I consider them one "set".

Character & text logos

I was inspired by the official art from the Connection era, which features distinct dark blue lineart with whimsically imperfect lines and flat colour.

Optionally, to match that, are some hand-drawn letters that match the character of the original Tamagotchi logo.

Characters only

(They are best friends. Do not separate.)

Why raccoons? Raccoons are THE unofficial symbol of Toronto and are pretty much the city's mascots. I've designed some playful and friendly characters that intend to capture the unique spirit of our city.

I propose that the Mametchi-esque character on the left is named Churronitchi (one of the local pronunciations of Toronto is "churrono"), and the Mimitchi-esque character on the right is named Binbintchi. Open to suggestions!
